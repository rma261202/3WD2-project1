Asian Menu

Gathering from all around region of Asia. From the East, Until the Southeastern, all the flavors that consist of exotic ingredients is ready to give you a great time..

Appetizer

Batagor

A dish consisting of fried fish dumplings, usually served with peanut sauce. It is traditionally made from minced tenggiri fish meat, although other types of seafood such as tuna, mackerel, and prawn may also be used.

Hiyayakko

A dish made with cold, uncooked tofu, cut into large squares and topped with condiments such as spring onions, ginger and dried bonito flakes and soy sauce.

Buchimgae

A dish made by pan-frying a thick batter mixed with egg and other ingredients until a thin flat pancake-shaped fritter is formed.

Steamed Haw Kou

A tasty crystal shrimp bonnet with its pleated shape

Main Course

Gyoza

crispy, tender Japanese dumplings—are filled with ground chicken or pork, ginger, mushrooms, and cabbage, then pleated and pan-fried before steaming.

Jeyum Bokkeum

A dish of beef marinated in a gochujang based sauce with lots of fresh garlic and ginger before grilled or pan-fried

Roast Duck

Roast Duck Recipe: juicy meat, crispy skin glazed with the honey-balsamic glaze

Opor Bebek

Dish that consist of tender duck meat that covered with curry broth that contains The spice mixture (bumbu) includes galangal, lemongrass, cinnamon, tamarind juice, palm sugar, coriander, cumin, candlenut, garlic, shallot, and pepper.

Dessert

Anmitsu

Classic meiji ere desserts that serve in a bowl with agar jelly and sweet azuki bean paste or anko (the an part of anmitsu), boiled peas, often gyūhi and a variety of fruits such as peach slices, mikan, pieces of pineapples, and cherries.

Bird Nest Soup

Delicate in presentation, Double-boiled Superior Bird Nest boasts of the rich taste and aroma of the bird’s nest paired with nutrient-rich egg yolk, creating a delicious medley of flavours

Mango Pudding

Heavenly mango pudding of Szechuan food style. This rich and silky dessert is bursting with luscious mango flavor. The simplicity of this pudding recipe makes it great for company.

Es Campur

Cold and sweet dessert concoction of fruit cocktails, coconut, tapioca pearls, grass jellies, etc. served in shaved ice, syrup and condensed milk.
