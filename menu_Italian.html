Italian Menu

Crafted from the tradition and famous technique with all of local imported products straight from Italy, it's guaranteed to give you the greatest italian experience.

Appetizer

Bruschetta

Classic Italian appetizer that consists with toasted bread that topped with tomatoes, Parmesan cheese, garlic, and fresh basil, with use of high-quality balsamic vinegar for best results.

Polenta Parmigiana

A traditional cornmeal loaf topped with tomato sauce and grated parmigiano reggiano with fresh basil and mozzarella

Focaccia Barese

Our authentic traditional bread that topped with charred tomatoes, olives, and oregano.

Marinated Mozzarella

Our fresh mozzarella marinated to our finest olive oil mixture that consists garlic. peppers, and herbs

Main Course

Neapolitana Pizza

Authentic Naples Style pizza. Baked traditio with our stone oven, with our signature dough and topped with our fresh basil and mozzarella.

Spaghetti Bolognese

A traditional pasta dishes, consists of spaghetti (long strings of pasta) with an Italian ragù (meat sauce) made with minced beef, bacon and tomatoes, served with Parmesan cheese.

Lasagna

A traditional pasta cuisine of stacked layers of lasagna alternating with fillings such as ragù (ground meats and tomato sauce), vegetables, cheeses (which may include ricotta, mozzarella, and parmesan), and seasonings and spices, like Italian seasoning, such as garlic, oregano and basil.

Seafood Risotto

Our northern Italian rice signature that use our fresh seafood with onion, white wine, and parmigiano reggiano.

Dessert

Pumpkin Gingersnaps Tiramisu

Our creation by fusioning Pumpkin pie and tiramisù, with layers of pumpkin-mascarpone custard and gingersnaps brushed with Calvados syrup. In the freezer, the flavors and textures meld to form a deliciously creamy dessert.

Chocolate Panna Cotta with Spiced Pepita Brittle

This light, silky panna cotta tastes a lot like hot cocoa in custard form. The brittle is easy to make; heat sugar and water on the stove, swirl in butter and spiced pepitas (shelled pumpkin seeds), then let cool

Sfogliatelle

The clam-shaped pastry of delicate thin outer layers filled with a mixture of sweetened ricotta cheese and citrus/orange peel (sometimes chocolate or cinnamon depending on the bakery), giving it just the right amount of sweetness.

Cannoli

The fried tube-shaped shells are filled with a sweetened ricotta cheese and sometimes dipped with chocolate, candied fruit, or pistachios.
