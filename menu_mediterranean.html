Mediterannean Menu

Created from around Greek, some parts of Italy and Spain, and including parts of Middle Eastern. It consist with lots of fruit, veggies, legumes, grains, olive oil and aromatic herbs.

Appetizer

Baba Ganoush

A Levantine appetizer consisting of finely chopped roasted eggplant, olive oil, lemon juice, various seasonings, and tahini. The eggplant is traditionally baked or broiled over an open flame before peeling, so that the pulp is soft and has a smoky taste.

Harcha

A bread in the form of a galette that is prepared with durum wheat flour, butter or olive oil and milk or water.

Main Course

Briam

Baked summer vegetables based on sliced potatoes and zucchini in olive oil. Usually includes eggplant, tomatoes, onions, and herbs and seasonings.

Koshari

A comforting bowl of spiced lentils and rice, combined with chickpeas and small pasta. All smothered in a tomato sauce that's been spiked with vinegar and topped with savory, crispy thin fried onion rings.

Beef Tagine

A famous Moroccan casserole, named after the clay dish it's cooked in, and containing strong, fragrant flavours and a tender juicy beef

Shakshuka

A dish of tender eggs poached in a perfectly spiced tomato based sauce.

Dessert

Crema Catalana

Smooth Spanish custard with a hint of citrus and spice, topped with a hardened caramel

Kanafeh

a traditional Middle Eastern dessert made with spun pastry called kataifi, soaked in a sweet, sugar-based syrup called attar, and typically layered with cheese, or with other ingredients such as clotted cream, pistachio or nuts

Krema Rezina

Lighter-than-air puff pastry floats atop vanilla whipped cream and a custard pastry cream, each slice trimmed to a perfect square.

Pasteis de Nata

A custard tart made with puff pastry and filled with egg custard that is served with a dusting of powdered sugar and a pinch of cinnamon.
